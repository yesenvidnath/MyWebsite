The Impact of Artificial Intelligence in Private Sector Education
K.K. Yesen Vidnath
Business & IT Research Conference (BIRC 2025)  ·  Sri Lanka  ·  2025
This research examines the adoption and impact of artificial intelligence tools within Sri Lanka's private tertiary education sector. The study investigates how AI technologies are reshaping pedagogical approaches, student learning outcomes, and institutional practices in private higher education institutions. Drawing on empirical data from educators and students, the paper analyses patterns of AI tool adoption, identifies barriers to integration, and evaluates the broader implications for educational quality and equity in the Sri Lankan context.
