cat bio.txt

Yesen is a Software Engineer and Lecturer with a strong foundation in web development and software engineering education. His professional journey has been shaped by hands-on industry experience, continuous self-driven learning, and a deep interest in applying technology to solve real-world problems.

His path into software engineering was non-linear — which played a significant role in developing resilience, adaptability, and practical thinking. These experiences strongly influence his approach to both development and teaching, where equal importance is placed on technical competence, logical reasoning, and professional discipline.

His technical focus lies primarily in backend development, system architecture, database design, and building scalable, maintainable solutions — while continuously exploring emerging technologies including Quantum Computing.

As a lecturer, Yesen's teaching philosophy centres on helping students move beyond theoretical knowledge to develop genuine problem-solving skills and an engineering mindset that prepares them for real-world challenges.

Driven by curiosity, resilience, and a commitment to continuous growth — his long-term vision is to support the development of capable, ethical, and industry-ready software engineers.
